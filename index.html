Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How often should I have my HVAC system serviced?

We recommend having your HVAC system serviced at least once a year. For optimal performance, it's best to schedule maintenance in the spring for your cooling system and in the fall for your heating system. Regular maintenance can improve efficiency, extend the lifespan of your equipment, and prevent costly repairs.

What are the signs that my AC needs repair?

Common signs include reduced cooling capacity, unusual noises, frequent cycling, higher energy bills, water leaks, and poor airflow. If you notice any of these issues, it's best to have a professional inspect your system. Early detection of problems can prevent more extensive damage and save you money in the long run.

How long does an HVAC installation typically take?

The duration depends on the complexity of the system and your property. A standard residential installation typically takes 1-2 days, while more complex commercial systems may require several days to complete. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your home or business while ensuring a quality installation.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency services for urgent HVAC issues. Our team is available to address critical problems that require immediate attention, especially during extreme weather conditions. We understand that HVAC emergencies can happen at any time, and we're committed to providing prompt, reliable service when you need it most.
